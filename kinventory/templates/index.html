Features

  • Tracking & managing stock and overall inventory.
  • Tracking & managing supply orders.
  • Tracking & managing suppliers.
  • Visual analytics on consumption and wastage.
  • Time series analysis based consumption prediction.
  • Automated supplier payments calculation.
  • A separate account for every user, secured with a password.
